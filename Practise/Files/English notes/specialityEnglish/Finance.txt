In finance, an option is a contract which gives the buyer (the owner or holder of the option) the right,
but not the obligation, to buy or sell an underlying asset or instrument at a specified strike price prior 
to or on a specified date.  
strike price: In finance, the strike price (or exercise price) of an option is the fixed price at which 
the owner of the option can buy (in the case of a call), or sell (in the case of a put), the underlying security or commodity.
